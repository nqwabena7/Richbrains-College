Admissions at Richbrains College

"Service to God and Humanity"

Why Choose Richbrains College?

At Richbrains College, we are committed to academic excellence and character development. Our goal is to equip students with both intellectual and moral discipline to serve God and humanity.

  • Dedicated and qualified teaching staff
  • Comprehensive curriculum across all major SHS programs
  • Supportive and nurturing learning environment
  • Emphasis on moral and spiritual growth
  • Modern teaching aids and practical learning approach

Admission Requirements

To enroll at Richbrains College, applicants must provide the following:

  • Completed admission form (available online or at the school office)
  • Two passport-sized photographs
  • BECE or equivalent result slip (for SHS 1 applicants)
  • Transfer or report card (for SHS 2 & SHS 3 applicants)
  • Birth certificate or any valid identification document

Admissions are open to students who are determined to learn, improve, and succeed. We believe in second chances and lifelong learning.
